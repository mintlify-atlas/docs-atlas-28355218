This guide will walk you through everything you need to become a powerful elemental wizard, from choosing your first school to mastering advanced spell combinations.

## Choose your elemental path

Before you begin, you'll need to decide which elemental school aligns with your playstyle:

  <Card title="Water" icon="droplet" color="#01d9cf">

  <Card title="Earth" icon="mountain" color="#bd8b00">

    Harness earth magic for powerful defensive buffs and devastating physical damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Air" icon="wind" color="#d5ebff">

<Note>
You can learn spells from multiple schools! The Elemental Staff provides spell power to all three elements.
</Note>

## Your first steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather crafting materials">
    Each spell book requires different materials:
    
    **Tome of the Aquamancer** (Water):
    - 1 Kelp
    - 1 Prismarine Crystals
    - 1 Book
    - 1 Lapis Lazuli
    
    **Tome of the Terramancer** (Earth):
    - 1 Pointed Dripstone
    - 1 Dripstone Block
    - 1 Book
    - 1 Lapis Lazuli
    
    **Tome of the Aeromancer** (Air):
    - 1 Phantom Membrane
    - 1 Feather
    - 1 Book
    - 1 Lapis Lazuli
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Craft your spell book">
    Use shapeless crafting to combine the materials for your chosen school. The spell book will grant you access to all spells in that element.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Obtain a weapon">
    Start with a basic wand or staff. Early options include:
    
    - **Wand (Tier 0)**: +3.0 spell power to your chosen element
    - **Staff (Tier 1)**: +4.0 spell power to your chosen element
    - **Elemental Staff**: +5.0 to ALL elemental schools (water, earth, air, fire)
    
    Check the [weapons](/equipment/weapons) page for crafting recipes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Equip armor">
    Basic Elemental Robes provide +20% spell power to all elements. They're the perfect starter armor for any wizard.
    
    See [armor sets](/equipment/armor-sets) for the full progression path.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Learn your first spells">
    With your spell book equipped, you'll automatically learn all spells for that school. Open your spell selection screen to choose which spells to equip.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding spell costs

Spells require resources to cast:

- **Elemental Stones**: Aqua Stone (water), Terra Stone (earth), Wind Stone (air)
- **Exhaust**: A fatigue meter that regenerates over time
- **Cooldowns**: Time before you can cast the spell again

<Tip>
Elemental stones can be found in loot chests or crafted. Always keep a stack in your inventory!
</Tip>

## Recommended starter spells

### Water path

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Aqua Splash (Tier 1)">
    A simple water projectile that deals damage and applies the Soaked effect. Perfect for learning spell mechanics.
    
    **Cost**: 15s cooldown, 0.1 exhaust
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Water Whip (Tier 2)">
    Strikes enemies in a line with water magic. Great for crowd control.
    
    **Cost**: 12s cooldown, 0.2 exhaust, 1 Aqua Stone
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Bubble Beam (Tier 2)">
    Channels healing in a cone area for yourself and allies while damaging enemies. Essential for survivability.
    
    **Cost**: 15s cooldown, 0.2 exhaust, 1 Aqua Stone
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Earth path

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stone Throw (Tier 1)">
    Hurls a stone projectile at your enemy. Simple but effective.
    
    **Cost**: 4s cooldown, 0.05 exhaust
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stone Flesh (Tier 1)">
    Grants +1.0 armor and +0.5 armor toughness for 16 seconds. Your primary defensive buff.
    
    **Cost**: 15s cooldown, 0.1 exhaust
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stone Spear (Tier 2)">
    Launches a powerful spear that applies bleeding to enemies. High damage potential.
    
    **Cost**: 18s cooldown, 0.2 exhaust, 1 Terra Stone
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Air path

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Gust (Tier 1)">
    Creates a wind blast that knocks back enemies. Essential for keeping distance.
    
    **Cost**: 6s cooldown, 0.1 exhaust
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Air Cutter (Tier 2)">
    Slashes enemies with compressed air. Fast casting and good damage.
    
    **Cost**: 12s cooldown, 0.2 exhaust, 1 Wind Stone
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Updraft (Tier 2)">
    Launches enemies into the air, making them vulnerable to air damage. Great setup for combos.
    
    **Cost**: 15s cooldown, 0.2 exhaust, 1 Wind Stone
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Combat tips

1. **Manage your cooldowns**: Don't waste powerful spells on weak enemies
2. **Combine elements**: Use the Elemental Staff to access multiple schools
3. **Status effects matter**: Updraft makes enemies vulnerable to air, Soaked increases lightning damage
4. **Armor synergies**: Specialized armor (Kelp, Dripstone, Wind) provides school-specific bonuses
5. **Carry elemental stones**: Always keep a stack of your primary element's stone

## Progression path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early game (Tier 1-2)">
    - Craft your spell book
    - Obtain Elemental Robes
    - Craft a basic wand or staff
    - Learn fundamental spells
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mid game (Tier 2-3)">
    - Upgrade to specialized armor (Kelp/Dripstone/Wind)
    - Craft higher tier weapons
    - Master advanced spells like Springwater or Terra Circle
    - Collect elemental stones
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Late game (Tier 3-5)">
    - Obtain Netherite-infused armor
    - Craft the Avatar Staff (if Arsenal mod is installed)
    - Unlock epic T5 armor sets with passive abilities
    - Master spell combos and synergies
  </Step>
</Steps>
