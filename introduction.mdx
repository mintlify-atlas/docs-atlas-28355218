Elemental Wizards is a Spell Engine add-on that brings elemental magic to Minecraft. Choose from three powerful elemental schools—Water, Earth, and Air—and unleash devastating spells upon your enemies.

## Three elemental schools

Master the ancient arts of elemental manipulation:

- **Water** - Heal allies and soak enemies with the power of flowing water
- **Earth** - Crush your foes with stone projectiles and defensive magic
- **Air** - Command the winds to knock back enemies and control the battlefield

## Features

### Powerful spell system

Access 18 unique elemental spells across three schools, from basic projectiles to advanced area-of-effect abilities. Each school has 6 spells with different tiers and effects.

### Specialized equipment

Craft wands, staves, and elemental robes to enhance your spell power. Upgrade your equipment from basic materials to netherite and beyond.

### Multiple armor tiers

- Tier 1: Elemental Apprentice armor (starter set)
- Tier 2: Element-specific armor (Aqua, Terra, Wind)
- Tier 3: Netherite armor variants
- Tier 5: Hurricane, Mountain, and Ocean sets with unique bonuses

### Cross-mod compatibility

Elemental Wizards integrates seamlessly with:

- More RPG Classes for expanded class options
- Better Combat for improved combat mechanics
- Arsenal (RPG Series) for additional weapons
- Spell Power and Spell Engine ecosystem
