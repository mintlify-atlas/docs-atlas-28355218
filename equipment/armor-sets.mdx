Elemental Wizards RPG features multiple tiers of magical armor sets, each providing unique spell power bonuses and special attributes.

## Armor Progression Overview

| Tier | Armor Sets | Protection (H/C/L/F) | Enchantability |
|------|------------|---------------------|----------------|
| T1 | Elemental Robes | 1/3/2/1 | 9 |
| T2 | Kelp, Dripstone, Wind | 1/3/2/1 | 10 |
| T3 | Netherite Kelp, Netherite Dripstone, Netherite Wind | 1/3/2/1 | 15 |
| T5 | Hurricane, Mountain, Ocean (Epic Robes) | 1/3/2/1 | 18 |

## Tier 1: Elemental Robes

The starter armor set that provides balanced bonuses to all elemental schools.

### Stats (Per Piece)
- **Spell Power Modifier**: +20% to all elemental schools
- **Schools**: Air, Earth, Water, Fire
- **Durability**: 10
- **Material**: Wool-based

### Attribute Bonuses

| Piece | Air Power | Earth Power | Water Power | Fire Power |
|-------|-----------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Helmet | +20% | +20% | +20% | +20% |
| Chestplate | +20% | +20% | +20% | +20% |
| Leggings | +20% | +20% | +20% | +20% |
| Boots | +20% | +20% | +20% | +20% |

**Full Set Bonus**: +80% spell power to all elemental schools

## Upgrading to Epic Robes

Epic T5 robes are obtained through smithing table upgrades:

- **Hurricane Robe**: Upgrade Wind or Netherite Wind armor pieces
- **Mountain Robe**: Upgrade Dripstone or Netherite Dripstone armor pieces  
- **Ocean Robe**: Upgrade Kelp or Netherite Kelp armor pieces

Smithing recipes can be found in the recipes directory for each upgrade path.

## Armor Material Properties

### Sound Effects
- **T1-T2 Armor**: Leather equip sound
- **T3+ (Netherite variants)**: Netherite equip sound
- **T5 (Epic Robes)**: Netherite equip sound

### Repair Materials
- **T1-T2**: Any color wool
- **T3-T5**: Netherite Ingot
