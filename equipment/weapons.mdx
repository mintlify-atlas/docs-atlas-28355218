Elemental Wizards RPG features two main weapon types: **Wands** (faster, lower damage) and **Staffs** (slower, higher damage). Each weapon provides spell power bonuses to specific elemental schools.

## Weapon Types Overview

| Type | Attack Damage | Attack Speed | Uses |
|------|---------------|--------------|------|
| Wand | 2 | -2.4 | Fast casting, mobility |
| Staff | 4 | -3.0 | High damage, powerful spells |

## Wands

Wands are lightweight weapons that provide moderate spell power bonuses. They have faster attack speed compared to staffs.

## Staffs

Staffs are powerful two-handed weapons that provide greater spell power bonuses but have slower attack speed.

### Base Stats
- **Attack Damage**: 4
- **Attack Speed**: -3.0
- **Category**: Damage Staff

### Multi-Element Staff

#### Elemental Staff (T1)
- **Spell Power**: +5 to ALL elements (Air, Earth, Water, Fire)
- **Material**: Diamond-tier
- **Repair**: Stick
- **Loot Tier**: 1

**Crafting**:
```java
  Q  (Q = Elemental Essence)
 S   (S = Stick)
S
```java

The Elemental Staff is unique as it provides bonuses to all four elemental schools, making it versatile for multi-school builds.

## Special Staffs

- **Spell Power**: +8 to Air, Earth, and Water
- **Material**: Netherite-tier
- **Repair**: Ambrosium Shard (Aether)
- **Loot Pool**: Aether dimension
- **Requires**: Aether mod (or config override)

Provides balanced bonuses to three elemental schools, perfect for hybrid builds.

### Unique Staff 1 (T5)
- **Spell Power**: +8 to Air, Earth, and Water
- **Material**: Netherite-tier
- **Repair**: Iron Block
- **Special Ability**: Grants Elemental Avatar spell
- **Loot Tier**: 5 (Crystal loot pool)
- **Rarity**: Rare
- **Requires**: Arsenal mod (or config override)

This legendary staff grants access to the powerful Elemental Avatar spell, allowing for ultimate elemental mastery.

## Spell Power Scaling

### Wand Progression
- **T0**: +3 Spell Power (Early game)
- **T1**: +4 Spell Power
- **T2**: +5 Spell Power (Mid game)
- **T3**: +5.5 Spell Power (Late game)

### Staff Progression
- **T1**: +5 Spell Power (Early-mid game)
- **T2**: +6 Spell Power (Mid game)
- **T3**: +7 Spell Power (Late game)
- **T4**: +8 Spell Power (End game, requires mod integration)

---

## Mod Integration

Some high-tier weapons require external mods for their full functionality:

- **Better End**: Crystal Aqua Staff, Aeternium Wind Staff
- **Better Nether**: Ruby Terra Staff
- **Aether**: Aether Staff
- **Arsenal**: Unique Staff 1 (with Elemental Avatar spell)

These requirements can be bypassed by setting `ignore_items_required_mods` to `true` in the mod configuration.
