Elemental Wizards supports both Fabric and NeoForge mod loaders for Minecraft 1.21.1.

## Requirements

<Note>
  Before installing Elemental Wizards, ensure you have the following:
  - Minecraft 1.21 or 1.21.1
  - Java 21 or higher
  - Either Fabric Loader 0.14.21+ or NeoForge 21.1.209+
</Note>

## Choose your platform

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Fabric">
    ### Fabric installation

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install Fabric Loader">
        Download and install [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/use/) version 0.14.21 or higher for Minecraft 1.21.1.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Download required dependencies">
        Download the following mods and place them in your `mods` folder:

        - [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api) 0.116.7+1.21.1 or higher
        - [AzureLib Armor](https://modrinth.com/mod/azurelibarmor) (any version)
        - [Spell Engine](https://modrinth.com/mod/spell-engine) 1.5.0 or higher
        - [Spell Power](https://modrinth.com/mod/spell-power) 1.2.0 or higher
        - [More RPG Classes](https://modrinth.com/mod/more-rpg-classes) 2.2.0-1.21.1 or higher
      </Step>

      <Step title="Download Elemental Wizards">
        Download Elemental Wizards version 2.5.4 for Fabric from [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/elemental-wizards-rpg) or [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/elemental-wizards-rpg).
      </Step>

      <Step title="Install the mod">
        Place the Elemental Wizards JAR file in your `mods` folder located in your Minecraft directory.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Launch Minecraft">
        Start Minecraft with the Fabric profile and verify that Elemental Wizards appears in your mod list.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Optional Fabric mods

    For enhanced gameplay, consider installing:

    - [Better Combat](https://modrinth.com/mod/better-combat) - Improved combat mechanics
    - [Trinkets](https://modrinth.com/mod/trinkets) - Additional equipment slots
    - [Mod Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu) - In-game mod configuration
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="NeoForge">
    ### NeoForge installation

    <Warning>
      NeoForge support is currently in **beta**. You may encounter bugs or compatibility issues.
    </Warning>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install NeoForge">
        Download and install [NeoForge](https://neoforged.net/) version 21.1.209 or higher for Minecraft 1.21.1.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Download required dependencies">
        Download the following mods and place them in your `mods` folder:

        - [AzureLib Armor](https://modrinth.com/mod/azurelibarmor) (any version)
        - [Spell Engine](https://modrinth.com/mod/spell-engine) 1.5.0 or higher (NeoForge version)
        - [Spell Power](https://modrinth.com/mod/spell-power) 1.2.0 or higher (NeoForge version)
        - [More RPG Classes](https://modrinth.com/mod/more-rpg-classes) 2.2.0-1.21.1 or higher (NeoForge version)
        - Forgified Fabric API 0.115.6+2.1.4+1.21.1
      </Step>

      <Step title="Download Elemental Wizards">
        Download Elemental Wizards version 2.5.4 for NeoForge from [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/elemental-wizards-rpg) or [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/elemental-wizards-rpg).
      </Step>

      <Step title="Install the mod">
        Place the Elemental Wizards JAR file in your `mods` folder located in your Minecraft directory.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Launch Minecraft">
        Start Minecraft with the NeoForge profile and verify that Elemental Wizards loads correctly.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Optional NeoForge mods

    For enhanced gameplay, consider installing:

    - [Better Combat](https://modrinth.com/mod/better-combat) - Improved combat mechanics
    - [Accessories](https://modrinth.com/mod/accessories) - Additional equipment slots
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Verify installation

Once you've installed Elemental Wizards and its dependencies:

1. Launch Minecraft
2. Create or load a world
3. Open your inventory
4. Look for the "Elemental Wizards" creative tab
5. Search for items like "Tome of Water" or "Aqua Wand"

If you can see these items, Elemental Wizards is installed correctly!

## Troubleshooting

### Missing dependencies error

If Minecraft fails to launch with a missing dependency error, ensure you've installed all required mods listed above with the correct versions.

### Version compatibility

Elemental Wizards 2.5.4 is built for Minecraft 1.21.1. Using it with other Minecraft versions may cause crashes or compatibility issues.

### NeoForge beta issues

If you experience issues with the NeoForge version, try the Fabric version instead, as it is the primary supported platform.
