# Earth Spells

Earth magic focuses on heavy physical damage, bleeding effects, and defensive buffs. All earth spells use **Terra Stones** as reagents and belong to the **EARTH** school.

## Tips for Earth Wizards

1. **Bleeding Immunity**: Undead enemies are immune to bleeding, so focus on raw damage against them
2. **Area Control**: Terra Circle creates dangerous zones that enemies must avoid
3. **Chain Reactions**: Shattering Stone becomes exponentially more powerful against grouped enemies
4. **No Cooldown Basics**: Stone Throw and Stone Spear can be spammed for consistent damage
5. **Defensive Options**: Stone Flesh provides crucial survivability in tough fights
6. **Range Advantage**: Earth spells have excellent range, especially Shattering Stone (38 blocks)
7. **Earthquake Positioning**: Use Earthquake when surrounded to clear enemies in all directions
