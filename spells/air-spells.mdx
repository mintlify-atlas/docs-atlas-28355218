## Tips for Air Wizards

1. **Boss Immunity**: Bosses are immune to crowd control effects but still take damage
2. **Positioning**: Use knockback to control enemy positioning and create distance
3. **Tornado Zones**: Place tornadoes strategically to deny areas and funnel enemies
4. **No Cooldown Basics**: Gust and Air Cutter can be used repeatedly for constant pressure
5. **Updraft Health Limit**: The health threshold means it works best on regular enemies, not elites
6. **Aeroburst Panic Button**: The massive knockback makes it perfect for escaping dangerous situations
7. **Vertical Control**: Air magic excels at vertical displacement, making it effective against ground-based enemies
8. **Channeled Spells**: Updraft and Tornado require commitment - position carefully before casting
