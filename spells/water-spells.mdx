# Water Spells

Water magic combines offensive and healing capabilities, making Water wizards versatile combatants. All water spells use **Aqua Stones** as reagents and belong to the **WATER** school.

## Tips for Water Wizards

1. **Soaked Synergy**: Many water spells apply Soaked, which increases enemy vulnerability to water damage
2. **Hybrid Role**: Use healing spells to support allies while damaging enemies
3. **Area Control**: Springwater and Explosive Bubbles provide excellent area coverage
4. **Sustained Damage**: Hydro Beam deals massive damage over time with its high coefficient
5. **No Cooldown Basics**: Splash and Water Whip can be cast repeatedly for consistent pressure
