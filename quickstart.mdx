This guide will help you get started with Elemental Wizards, from choosing your elemental school to casting your first spells.

## Choose your elemental school

Elemental Wizards offers three distinct magical paths, each with unique spells and playstyles:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Water" icon="droplet">
    Versatile healing and damage. Splash heals allies and damages enemies, while advanced spells like Hydro Beam apply the Soaked effect.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Earth" icon="mountain">
    Powerful projectile damage and defense. Stone Throw deals area damage, while Stone Flesh provides armor and damage reduction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Air" icon="wind">
    Crowd control and knockback. Gust pushes enemies away, while Tornado summons devastating cyclones that lift foes into the air.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  You're not locked into a single school—you can learn spells from multiple elements and even use the Elemental Staff to cast all three types!
</Note>

## Craft your first spell book

To begin your elemental journey, you'll need a Tome (spell book) for your chosen school.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather materials">
    Each tome requires:
    - 1 Book
    - 1 Lapis Lazuli
    - 2 element-specific items

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Water">
        **Tome of Water** requires:
        - Kelp
        - Prismarine Crystals
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Earth">
        **Tome of Earth** requires:
        - Obsidian
        - Dripstone Block
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Air">
        **Tome of Wind** requires:
        - Wind Charge
        - Gold Ingot
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Craft the tome">
    Place all four ingredients in a crafting table using shapeless crafting. The order doesn't matter.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Learn your spells">
    Right-click while holding the tome to learn all basic spells for that element. The tome will be consumed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Obtain a weapon

You'll need a wand or staff to cast your spells. Each element has its own weapon progression.

### Starting weapons

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Water">
    **Kelp Wand** - Basic water weapon

    Craft with kelp and basic materials. Provides water spell power to cast your aqua spells.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Earth">
    **Clay Wand** - Basic earth weapon

    Craft with clay and basic materials. Provides earth spell power to cast your terra spells.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Air">
    **Feather Wand** - Basic air weapon

    Craft with feathers and basic materials. Provides air spell power to cast your wind spells.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Weapon progression

As you advance, you can craft more powerful weapons:

1. **Wands** (Tier 1) - Kelp, Clay, Feather
2. **Element Wands** (Tier 2) - Aqua, Terra, Wind
3. **Netherite Wands** (Tier 3) - Netherite variants
4. **Staves** (Tier 2-4) - More powerful, two-handed weapons
5. **Special Staves** - Crystal, Ruby, Aeternium variants with unique bonuses

<Note>
  The **Elemental Staff** provides spell power for all three elements, allowing you to cast water, earth, and air spells with a single weapon.
</Note>

## Cast your first spell

Now that you have a spell book and weapon, you're ready to cast spells!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Equip your weapon">
    Hold your wand or staff in your main hand. Your spell power will appear in your stats.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open spell selection">
    Press the spell selection key (default: **R**) to open your spell wheel or list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a spell">
    Choose one of your learned spells. Each school starts with basic tier 0 spells:

    - **Water**: Splash (heal/damage spell)
    - **Earth**: Stone Throw (projectile)
    - **Air**: Gust (knockback)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cast the spell">
    Right-click (use item) while holding your weapon to cast the selected spell. Watch for the casting animation and particles!
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Spell costs

Most spells require **elemental stones** to cast:

- Water spells use **Aqua Stones**
- Earth spells use **Terra Stones**
- Air spells use **Storm Stones**

Higher tier spells also have cooldowns and may cost exhaust (hunger).

## Learn advanced spells

Each elemental school has 6 spells ranging from tier 0 (basic) to tier 4 (expert).

### Water spells

1. **Splash** (Tier 0) - Heal allies or damage enemies
2. **Water Whip** (Tier 1) - Fast damage that soaks targets
3. **Bubble Beam** (Tier 2) - Damage enemies and heal/protect allies
4. **Springwater** (Tier 3) - Summon healing springs
5. **Hydro Beam** (Tier 3) - Channeled high-pressure beam
6. **Explosive Bubbles** (Tier 4) - Area damage and healing

### Earth spells

1. **Stone Throw** (Tier 0) - Projectile with area damage
2. **Stone Spear** (Tier 1) - Projectile that inflicts bleeding
3. **Stone Flesh** (Tier 2) - Defensive buff with damage reduction
4. **Terra Circle** (Tier 3) - Dripstone circles that damage and bleed
5. **Earthquake** (Tier 3) - Area damage that throws enemies
6. **Shattering Stones** (Tier 4) - Projectile that splits on impact

### Air spells

1. **Gust** (Tier 0) - Basic wind damage
2. **Air Cutter** (Tier 1) - Damage with knockback
3. **Aeroblast** (Tier 2) - Knock enemies into the air
4. **Updraft** (Tier 3) - Continuous damage, increased while airborne
5. **Tornado** (Tier 3) - Summon a cyclone that pulls and damages
6. **Aeroburst** (Tier 4) - Circular knockback burst

### How to learn advanced spells

Advanced spells can be obtained through:

- **Spell Scrolls** - Found in loot chests (beginner, expert, and treasure variants)
- **Villager trading** - Wizard villagers may offer spell scrolls
- **Crafting** - Some spell scrolls can be crafted (check JEI/REI)

<Warning>
  Advanced spells require higher spell power to be effective. Make sure to upgrade your weapon and armor as you progress!
</Warning>

## Craft elemental armor

Enhance your spell power and survivability with elemental armor sets.

### Armor tiers

1. **Elemental Apprentice** (Tier 1) - Basic starting armor
2. **Element-specific** (Tier 2) - Aqua, Terra, or Wind armor
3. **Netherite variants** (Tier 3) - Enhanced durability and stats
4. **Elite sets** (Tier 5) - Hurricane, Mountain, and Ocean sets

Each full armor set provides:
- Increased spell power for your element
- Armor and toughness stats
- Set bonuses (for tier 5 sets)

## Tips for success

- **Combine elements**: Use spells from multiple schools to adapt to different situations
- **Watch your resources**: Keep a good supply of elemental stones for spell casting
- **Soaked synergy**: Water spells deal bonus damage to fire mobs and apply Soaked, which makes targets vulnerable
- **Crowd control**: Air spells excel at controlling groups—use Aeroburst or Tornado when surrounded
- **Defensive play**: Earth's Stone Flesh can save your life by reducing incoming damage by 50% at full health
- **Boss immunity**: Some spell effects like knockup don't work on bosses (entities tagged `#c:bosses`)

## Next steps

You're now ready to explore the world as an elemental wizard! Consider:

- Experimenting with spell combinations from different schools
- Hunting for treasure spell scrolls in dungeons and structures
- Crafting the **Avatar's Staff** (Tier 5) for unique elemental passive effects
- Installing compatible mods like Better Combat or Arsenal for expanded gameplay
